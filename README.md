## About

Five Nights At The Experiments is a fan-made horror game inspired by the Five Nights at Freddy's series. Take on the role of a night guard at a mysterious research facility where experiments have gone horribly wrong. Survive five nights of terror as you monitor cameras, manage limited resources, and avoid the twisted creations lurking in the shadows.

## Gameplay

- **Survive the Night**: Monitor security cameras and keep track of the experiments as they move through the facility
- **Manage Resources**: Balance your power usage carefully - running out means certain doom
- **Stay Alert**: React quickly to threats and use your tools wisely to make it through each shift
- **Uncover the Story**: Piece together what happened at this facility through environmental clues and hidden lore

## Features

- Atmospheric horror gameplay with strategic resource management
- Multiple nights with increasing difficulty
- Unique experiment creatures with distinct behaviors and patterns
- Immersive sound design and visuals
- Hidden secrets and lore to discover
